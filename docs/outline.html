ISSUES IN PAKISTAN ECONOMY

COURSE DESCRIPTION

This course gives an overview of economic issues of Pakistan. Starting from national income accounts, it carries a detail description of all sectors of the economy. Moreover it also highlights the main problems like poverty, inflation and unemployment. The last section of this course describes the recent challenges to the economy of Pakistan.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

The major objective of the course is to give the students the complete comprehension o socioeconomic issues of Pakistan economy. The students will be able: 1. To have the familiarity with key features of socioeconomic problems and their solutions in Pakistan 2. To understand the process of emerging issues in Pakistan economy 3. To analyze the programs dealing with issues in Pakistan economy

LEARNING OUTCOMES

Upon Successful Completion, the Students Should be able to: - IDENTIFY the challenges to the economy of Pakistan - UNDERSTAND the problems to the economy of Pakistan - HAVE knowledge about the state of economy.
